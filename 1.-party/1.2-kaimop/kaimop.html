History

Pre-Campaign

???? (?? years ago)

  • Before getting kicked out, Kaimop spent most of his time either working at his family’s tavern, mining iron with Dartanian, or doing odd jobs around the neighborhood.
  • A pastime not relating to work that Kaimop liked to do was repainting the signposts around his neighborhood. A contract miner, he only mined if he was called in. 

6559 (25 years ago)

  • Jasper found Kaimop near an abandoned railroad station in Copper Lane, Anemone. 

6566 (18 years ago)

  • Kaimop, Malba, and Dartanian found signposts for the first time.
  • Kaimop was 7 years old at this time.

6575 (9 years ago)

  • Kaimop met Mariana and she became, essentially, his second mother. Eventually, he confronted her about the local rumors that he was her child. She denied it, but they kept the ruse amongst their neighbors.
  • Dartanian started working in the mines.
  • Kaimop was 16 years old at this time.

6578 (6 years ago)

  • Gabriel, Mariana’s fiance, died in a mining accident. 
  • Kaimop was 19 years old at this time.

6581 (3 years ago)

  • York arrived in town and became fast friends with Kaimop.
  • Kaimop was 22 years old at this time.

6584 (3 to 4 months ago)

  • Kaimop’s family essentially kicked him out of the house and tell him to discover something more to life than just working. They view him as an empty shell that needs to be filled with something more than just a work ethic. They think Kaimop needs to live a better life than what he’s currently living.

During Campaign

Residency

Family-Owned Tavern

  • The home is located behind the family-owned tavern. One story with five modest bedrooms (each bedroom includes a bed, drawers & a small closet). The living/dining room is the centerpiece of the home and is decently sized.
  • Jasper/Perna, Becian/Drea, Malba, and Peony each have rooms, and Calca has a crib set up in Kaimop’s room.
  • The small rooms don’t bother anyone, and usually, a tavern bedroom is reserved for whoever works the night shift.

Jasper (3) - father

Appearance:

  • Human, 55 years old
  • Light brown hair and dark brown eyes. Has a clean look about him and wears quality clothes for the area.

Personality:

  • Calm, collected, and is seen as a nice guy in the community. Is a known haggler. 

Backstory:

  • Before meeting Perna, he was known for running the streets and getting into all sorts of stupid trouble. When he wasn’t running around he worked as the bartender at his family’s tavern.
  • After meeting Perna, he eventually fell in love with her eagerness/love of adventure and married her. The neighbors know about him and his wife in bed and even ask Jasper for tips. All but one (Kaimop) of his three children know.

Perna (4) - mother

Appearance:

  • Human, 53 years old
  • Light sandy hair and light brown eyes. Dresses cleanly and functionally. 

Personality:

  • Bubbly, loud, and straightforward, yet surprisingly gentle when need be.

Backstory:

  • Perna traveled up north from Brasscroft looking for adventure. Quickly, she found herself broke and spent her days at a tavern in Copper Lane.
  • The tavern’s bartender, Jasper, caught her eye, but she gave up pursuing him at first when he didn’t see her romantically. She eventually got a job at that tavern as a cook and made Copper Lane her home. The bartender fell in love with her and married her.

Friends/Others

Mariana (1) - maternal figure

Appearance

  • Human, 42 years old
  • Sandy blond hair and pale brown eyes. Wears clothes that are well worn and well-loved.

Personality:

  • Quiet and soft-spoken around most folks. Becomes visibly nervous in unfamiliar situations, but can push through most of the time.

Background:

  • Mariana has lived in Copper Lane all her life, and is locally known as the woman who sold her newborn baby to a noble. Neighbors assumed that Kaimop was that newborn baby because he was often seen trailing after her when he was younger.
  • Eventually, he confronted her about the rumors, and she assured him that he was not her biological son. She begged Kaimop to keep the truth a secret because it would be easier for everyone, and he agreed. A few years later, Kaimop was her biggest support when her fiance died in the mines. During this time, Kaimop learned about the abuse she suffered from her ex-husband years before she met her fiance.

Dartanian (2) - best-friend

Appearance:

  • Half-Orc, 22 years old
  • Dark green skin, black wavy hair, and gray eyes. Has a boyish charm to him and dresses neatly.

Personality:

  • Naturally boisterous and is known to give into his chaotic tendencies.

Background:

  • Started working in the copper mines when he was 16 years old, and has a history/backstory commonly seen in Copper Lane. He has known Kaimop’s family for years, and has pinned for Peony for almost as long as he’s known her.
  • When they were younger, Kaimop, Malba and him were the three musketeers. The trio would often be seen redesigning the signs all over the neighborhood. Soon after Kaimop left, Dartanian gently turned down Malba’s affections, and hasn’t seen much of her since. He talks to Peony the most out of Kaimop’s family now, but they really only met to recolor signs. He seems quite lonely.

York (3) - work best-friend

Appearance:

  • Human, 28 years old
  • Bronze skin, black curly hair, and light brown eyes.
  • No matter what he wears, there always seems to be oil/grease stains on it. (Leo Valdez aesthetic.) 

Personality:

  • A man of few words. Gruff, but incredibly kind. Values loyalty and familial bonds. Can be very talkative when he gets passionate about a subject. 

Backstory:

  • York moved from his home district of {blank}, 3 years before the start of the campaign due to his parents’ passing. Before working in the mines, he was the local handyman in his neighborhood.
  • Soon after moving to Copper Lane, York met Kaimop while mining and became fast friends. They could go a whole day without saying a word to each other and they enjoy their shared silence. Before Kaimop started his journey, York offered to join him but Kaimop told him to stay home to watch over everyone. After having her advances rejected by Dartanian, Malba has started hanging out with York.
