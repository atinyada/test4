Residency

Wynn’s Family Home

  • Humble, old little home that has seen better days. Two stories with a basement. Kinda looks like the home of a really nice grandma: old but comforting, and filled with trinkets. Wynn’s bedroom looks like a time capsule from her teenage years, notably full of filled diaries, musings, her favorite books, crafts, and a homemade shrine to the Tempest that looks like it was made by a child.
