History

Pre-Campaign

6557 - 6566

  • Irosys remembers playing tag with his sister and cousins.
  • Irosys remembers being dolled up by his mother for their family picture day.
  • Irosys faintly remembers his father's affections towards him.

6566

  • One morning, with the help of his cousin, he snuck out of his quarters and explored beyond his family's estate for the first time. While he was exploring, his was lured away from a busy street and was kidnapped by a seemingly sweet woman.
  • Irosys was then indoctrinated into the LoA and enrolled into its young development program. Through this program Irosys was placed on a team with Talia and Dusan, and they were mentored by Gladva.
  • Irosys was 9 years old at this time.

6579 - Start of Campaign

  • As part of a long-term mission, Irosys was allowed to return to his family and reintigrate back into high society. He approached his sister and her guards while at the local market plaza in Diamond Heights. He proved his legitimacy by showing his version of their family ring, and he was rushed back to the estate.
  • His mother and sister immediately welcomed him back into the family, but it took much longer for the remaining close family to do the same. It's only recently that Irosys has been invited to family gatherings, and allowed to socialize unattended at high-profile events.
  • Any inquiries into the years before his return were met with vague answers and deflections from his mother.
